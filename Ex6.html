CS 497

Course Description

Study of selected topics in computer and/or information systems management which are not covered in other course offerings, such as operating systems, case studies, exotic programming languages, compilers, microcomputer systems, integrated software, database design, networking, artificial intelligence and expert systems, computer algebra systems, etc. Students are expected to complete several laboratory assignments. CS497 teaches students how to develop applications with ReactJS and React Native.

Learning Objectives

By the end of the semester, students will be able to:
    Understand and demonstrate an understanding of basic HTML & CSS.
  • Understand the JavaScript language features, including classes, modules, and arrow functions
  • Explore the basic architecture of a React application
  • Develop a simple application with React JS/React Native

Course Methodology and Requirements

This course will be conducted in a hybrid mode. Classes on Wednesdays and Fridays will be conducted in-person, other days will be conducted synchronously online via Blackboard Collaborate Ultra. All learning materials will be posted on Blackboard. All assignments and exercises will be submitted via Blackboard. Students are expected to be present live for in-class quizzes (if any), midterm and final exams. If you are unable to attend class due to an emergency, please notify the instructor as soon as you know. Students are expected to participate in class discussion and complete in-class labs and exercises.

Assignments and Course Projects

Assignments and course projects include both written and programming tasks. Detailed instructions for each assignment will be posted on the Blackboard. Assignments are due by 11.59pm on the designated due date. Assignments turned in after the deadline date and time will be penalized by a deduction of 15% per day, if submitted within 48 hours of the deadline. Assignments submitted more than 48 hours late will receive 0 points.

Make-up quiz/test Policy

Attendance at quizzes/exams is mandatory. Make-up quizzes/exams will only be given in the case of emergency, such as illness (with a doctor’s note). In such case, you must email the instructor as soon as possible preferably before the quizzes/exams. A make-up quiz can only be arranged for students with a valid reason, otherwise they will receive 0 point for that exam.
